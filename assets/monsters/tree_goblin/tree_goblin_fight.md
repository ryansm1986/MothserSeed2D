##Tree Goblin

Abilities

1) Head throw: Uses head_throw animation, pauses midway. and the spinning_head projectile flies in circles around and the circle gets and it does faster and bigger circles. Lasts 5 seconds and flies back to tree_goblin and the animation resumes with puttin the head back on
2) Arm attack: Slightly longer range melee arm attack in a cone in front of the tree goblin

AI:

Prioritizes head throw. 15 second cooldown.
Attacks with arm attack when in range of player.
