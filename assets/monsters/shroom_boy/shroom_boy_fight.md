##Shroom Boy

Abilities

1) Spore cloud: Sprays poison cloud into thare and homes in slowly to the character. Lasts 10 seconds. Has 12 second cooldown.
2) Shroom toss: Tosses a mini version of itself toward the player and hits the ground and crawls and attacks player. Lasts 5 seconds.
3) Bite: Bites the player when they are close. Small cone shape.

AI:

Starts fight with spore cloud and prioritizes spore cloud.
Shroom toss if the player is out of melee range. 6 second cooldown.
Bite melee attack when in range. 3 second cooldown.
